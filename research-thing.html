Introducing LabCap

Crowdsourcing for your research.

Take a Demo Read Summary

Intro

Intro

-> LabCap allows research institutes or individual researchers to crowdsource funds to do their research.
-> It’s primary audience is underfunded research institutes.
-> It helps excel and progress scientific research forward and removes the barrier of budgets from scientific discoveries.



Problem Statement

  • Limited Funding -> A low amount of funding for researchers, results in them fighting for funds to do basic science. Decline in funding quantity and pressure to publish drives conflicts of interest and overhyping of results.
    -> Strains research quality, undermines objectivity, and influences researchers to prioritize quantity over substance.

  • Lack of discoverability: -> There is a general lack of visibility for the type of researches that happen in each institute or done by an individual researcher.
    -> This leads to no to limited collaboration and less impactful research.
    -> Funders don’t have visibility too, leading to low funding in certain areas.

Personas

Personas



Let’s define some user personas that are primary targets for this app:

Research Institute

“Our goal is to conduct high-impact research, generating new knowledge, and disseminating this knowledge through publications in top journals and conferences.”

Goals:
-> Secure Funding: Primary goal is to secure funding for ongoing and upcoming research projects.
-> Visibility: Increase visibility for their research projects and initiatives.
-> Collaboration: Find potential collaborators and partners for joint research efforts.
-> Streamlined Process: Simplify the funding application and management process for efficiency.

Frustrations:
-> Limited Funding Options: Difficulty in finding diverse funding sources.
-> Lack of Visibility: Struggle to showcase the institute's research projects to a broader audience.
-> Tedious Application Process: Cumbersome and time-consuming funding application procedures.

Need:
Research institute is trying to access a broader pool of funders and want more visibility to increase collaborations. They also want efficiency in funding processes.

Individual Researcher - Rohan

“I want to be ‘the’ inventor and help people with my inventions.”

Goals:
-> Financial Support: Secure funding for their research projects.
-> Networking: Build a professional network for potential collaborations.
-> Visibility: Increase visibility for their research work and achievements.

Frustrations:
-> Limited Funding Opportunities: Struggle to find suitable funding sources for their research.
-> Isolation: Difficulty in connecting with other researchers or institutions for collaboration.
-> No such platform exists to help with this.

Need:
Access to a variety of funding sources resulting in financial support for research endeavors. Build a network of collaborators and mentors for ongoing and future research projects. Easily manage and track funding activities, allowing more time for actual research.

Individual Researcher - Rohan

“I want to fund scientific endeavours out of my extra cash and help out individuals perform their research effectively.”

Goals:
-> Impactful Giving: Contribute to research projects aligned with personal interests or social impact.
-> Transparency: Ensure transparency in how funds are utilized by researchers or institutions.
-> Discoverability: Easily discover and support innovative research initiatives.

Frustrations:
-> Lack of Information: Difficulty in finding detailed and credible information about research projects.
-> Limited Options: Limited visibility into a diverse range of research projects to support.
-> Complex Processes: Frustration with convoluted donation processes.

Need:
Support research projects that align with their interests and values. Discover a wide range of research initiatives, contributing to a more informed decision-making process. Experience a user-friendly and transparent platform for supporting research, fostering a sense of satisfaction in their philanthropic endeavors.

Technology required

Tech Stack for Development

Frontend
Flutter: quick to develop and implement android apps as well as iOS apps. A lot of modules available to help speed up the process of app development.

Backend
Node.js: Node.js will be flexible for this project to use to set up the backend. Other alternatives are Flask, Django, etc. that can be used by developers not that procifienct with JavaScript.

Database
MySQL or PostGres: Both provide good relational database functionality for the type of user data we will store.

Cloud Infra
AWS: AWS provides a lot of flexibility while scaling as well as AWS has a lot of applications for cloud that will help us better deploy our services. From security to APIs to ML processing to storage everything can to handled by AWS.
