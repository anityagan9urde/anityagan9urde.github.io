What you can include as a researcher

Think of your LabCap project card as the front of your project. It should be easy to scan in less than a minute and make someone say “I get what this is about.”

  • A short, plain‑language title that avoids heavy jargon.
  • A one‑sentence description that explains the core idea and why it matters.
  • Names and roles of key team members, so visitors know who is behind the work.
  • A simple breakdown of how funds will be used and what milestones you plan to hit.

What a funder or supporter looks for

Most people skim the first screen before deciding whether to scroll further or support. Your project card is there to answer a few simple questions clearly.

  • What is this project about and who is behind it?
  • How will their money be used and what could it unlock?
  • How long will the project run and when will they hear back?
  • Are there clear signs that the team and institute are credible?
